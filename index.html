I help turn AI tools and agentic AI ideas into practical workflows for research, reporting, drafting, QA, ecommerce operations, intake, documentation, and internal execution. The goal is not novelty — it is a process people can actually use, review, and improve.

Yes. I help map which spreadsheets drive decisions, identify the source of truth, remove duplicate manual entry, standardize key fields, and turn messy data into dashboards, reports, or lightweight data platforms.

Yes. My experience includes Amazon, eBay, inventory, product data, listing workflows, fulfillment, warehouse operations, marketplace reporting, and ecommerce process improvement.

Yes, when they are the right tool. I can help design Custom GPTs, GPT-style assistants, agentic AI workflows, prompt systems, routing steps, and human review loops, but I treat them as one part of a broader operational system.

Does Yonatan offer tutoring or one-on-one technical help?

Yes. I can help individuals, students, founders, operators, and teams understand AI tools, troubleshoot workflows, choose software, build projects, improve SOPs, or get unstuck.

Is Yonatan available for remote consulting or contract work?

Yes. I am based in North Miami Beach, Florida and am open to remote work, consulting, contract projects, technical operations work, implementation support, and practical project help.
